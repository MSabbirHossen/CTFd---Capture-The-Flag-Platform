Al Khwarizmi CTF Contest

Registration Closed

A dynamic cybersecurity competition designed to test the skills of problem-solvers and digital defenders. Participants engage with real-world scenarios in a safe, ethical environment, challenging their critical thinking and technical expertise. The contest promotes teamwork, learning, and hands-on experience in cyber defense.

Inspired by Al-Khwarizmi's legacy of logic and innovation, this event blends knowledge, strategy, and creativity.
